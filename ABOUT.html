ABOUT US

Concerned Youth For Nature Initiative (COYNi) was started in 2018 by Mr MUSINGUZI JOSEPH
as World wide environment initiative to end plastic pollutionin the World.

COYNi is an official "Stichting" (Public Benefit Organisation, charity) according to the Ugandan law with a Board.
The bylaws meet the conditions for Non Governmental Organisations (NGO's).

STATUTORY OBJECTIVE

The objective of COYNi is to contribute with other climate initiative to the reduction of plastic pollution in the world.
